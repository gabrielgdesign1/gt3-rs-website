03 — Aerodynamics

The air is a component.

A·01

Porsche Active Aerodynamics

The front diffuser and rear wing adjust automatically to the driving situation — in fractions of a second. Full downforce for the corner. Every corner.

A·02

Drag Reduction System

Straight ahead, the wing flattens and air resistance collapses. DRS — technology carried straight over from top-flight motorsport.

A·03

S-Duct & central radiator

Like a GT racing car, one large central radiator sits where the front luggage compartment used to be. The S-shaped airflow gives the concept its name — and the brakes their breath.

A·04

Aero blades & wide body

Blades on the front axle add up to 40 kg of downforce at top speed. The wide Turbo body with side intakes makes room for wider wheels — and a sharper attitude.

Headwind is the biggest rival on the hunt for lap times. The GT3 RS uses it — on every single component.

04 — The engine

Setting the pace:
0rpm

The 4.0-litre naturally aspirated boxer six revs like a race engine because it is one — four-valve tech, rigid valve drive, forged pistons, titanium connecting rods and dry-sump lubrication with a separate oil tank. Individual throttle valves feed it. A lightweight stainless-steel sport exhaust lets it speak. Unfiltered.

06 — Chassis

Maximum performance, wheel by wheel.

  • D·01

    PTV Plus

    Porsche Torque Vectoring Plus with a fully variable, electronically controlled rear differential lock — more traction, more lateral punch, more stability under load change.

  • D·02

    PASM & PSM

    Active Suspension Management regulates damping force at every single wheel. Stability Management keeps it all pointed where you're looking.

  • D·03

    Rear-axle steering

    Agility in slow corners, unshakable calm at speed — the rear wheels steer with you, into the apex and past the field.

  • D·04

    Motorsport brakes

    Six-piston aluminium monobloc calipers up front, four-piston at the rear. Optional PCCB ceramics for even less weight and even later braking points.

  • D·05

    Track-wide chassis

    Ball-jointed chassis mounts, anti-dive geometry and a wide track — optimized for the circuit, faithful on the road.

Light leaves. Downforce stays.
